<h1>Why eGuarantees?</h1>
<p>Today, businesses and individuals (applicants) apply for or purchase banker's
guarantees and insurance bonds from financial institutions (FIs) such as
banks and insurers. They then deliver the paper-form of these guarantees
and bonds to Government Agencies to meet contractual or licensing requirements.
eGuarantee@Gov digitises this process to enable applicants, FIs and Agencies
to reap time and cost savings.</p>
<h1>Benefits of eGuarantee@Gov</h1>
<ol>
<li>
<p><strong>Swift</strong> - Fast issuance process, usually less than 1 day;
<a href="#_ftn1" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">[1]</a>
</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Seamless</strong> - eGuarantee lodged and discharged on Customs
eServices via a digitalised process; and</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Savings</strong> - No need for courier charges and costs for physical
storage of paper guarantees.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<hr>
<p><a href="#_ftnref1" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">[1]</a> Subject
to other bank conditions such as credit limit.</p>
