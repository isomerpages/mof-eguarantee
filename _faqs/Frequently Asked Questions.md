<h1>Frequently Asked Questions</h1>
<h3>What are Guarantees?</h3>
<p>Guarantees are issued by Financial Institutions as an undertaking that
the business or individual will fulfill its contractual or licensing obligations/
regulatory requirements. They can be in the form of a banker's guarantee,
insurer's guarantee or insurer's bond.</p>
<p>Guarantees are used by the Government:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>As security deposits and tender deposits for procurement, revenue and
land sales contracts; and</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>For licensing obligations/ regulatory requirements, e.g. employment agency
licensing, dog breed licensing, GST registration.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h3>Why should I apply for an eGuarantee instead of a paper guarantee?</h3>
<p>You can enjoy a faster lodgement process. Typically, it takes you up to
5-7 days from application to delivery of the guarantee to the Agency. With
eGuarantee@Gov, the application and issuance process can be completed within
a day. You can also enjoy cost savings as there is no longer a need to
collect the paper guarantee from the bank or insurance company and deliver
them to the Agency.</p>
<h3>What type of eGuarantee can I apply for?</h3>
<p>You can apply for guarantees for i) procurement of general goods and services
and construction services, ii) revenue contracts, iii) land sale tenders,
and iv) licensing obligations/ regulatory requirements. Please approach
the Agency you are dealing with to find out the type of eGuarantee it requires.</p>
<h3>How do I check on the status of my eGuarantee? Who should I contact regarding the status of my eGuarantee application.</h3>
<p>The bank or insurance company will inform you that it has issued the eGuarantee.
Once your eGuarantee has been lodged successfully, the Agency will inform
you on the next step of your procurement, revenue contracts, land sale
tenders or licensing process. You may contact the Agency regarding the
status of your eGuarantee if it has been lodged successfully.</p>
<h3>How is eGuaranee@Gov different from the Electronic Bankers’ Guarantee Programme?</h3>
<p>eGuarantee@Gov is an expansion of the Electronic Bankers’ Guarantee Programme
used by Singapore Customs. It will tap on the Customs eServices and include
more types of guarantees required by government agencies. More Financial
Institutions (FIs) will also participate so applicants can have more choices
of the FI they wish to purchase eGuarantees from. FIs will also benefit
from greater participation by applicants.</p>
<h3>What is Customs eServices?</h3>
<p>Customs eServices is Singapore Customs' digital services platform that
enables businesses and individuals to access and transact with Customs
services online. It provides a one-stop portal for permit applications,
declarations, certifications, and other regulatory services.</p>
